Hasitha Kusam ‒ Light Opera: Patience (Part 2)

Patience, or Bunthorne’s Bride

Act I, continuing

(Later that day; the suite assembled again.)

DUK

We shall see whether Bunthorne’s vanity can endure such loyal affection.

BUN

Reginald Bunthorne, I fear, becomes the prisoner of applause.

PAT

I never sought admiration, yet here it is, unwelcome and loud.

(They depart.)
