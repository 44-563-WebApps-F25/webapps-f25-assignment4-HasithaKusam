Hasitha Kusam - Light Opera

The Mikado

Act I, Scene I

(A public square in the town of Titipu. Gentlemen of Japan discovered.)

NAN

A wandering minstrel I— a thing of shreds and patches.

CHO

Of ballads, songs, and snatches, and dreamy lullaby!

NAN

My catalogue is long, through every passion ranging.

CHO

And to your humbler, weaker thoughts, I’ll lend an easy cry.

NAN

I tune my supple song to suit the vocal strength of others.

CHO

When anguish wrings the heart, or when the soul is full of laughter!

NAN

For he who’d charm both old and young must make his music matter!

(They bow ceremoniously.)
